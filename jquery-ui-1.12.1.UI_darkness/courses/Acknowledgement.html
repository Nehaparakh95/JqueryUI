Spartan Food Pantry

The Spartan Food Pantry is a full-service, staffed, walk-in pantry at SJSU offering non-perishable goods, fresh produce, and refrigerated items.Spartan Food Pantry Logo No student should have to face food insecurity. We define those in need as students who have limited or uncertain access to nutritious and safe foods because of a lack of money and other resources. Much of our food provided is donated in partnership with Second Harvest of Silicon Valley. Individual donations are accepted during operational hours.

Eligibility: Experiencing Hunger/Food Insecurity
Must be currently enrolled or future enrolled SJSU student who has had prior enrollment
Must bring Tower ID Card and reusable grocery bags
Must earn a gross annual income of $33,348* or less (no documentation required)
